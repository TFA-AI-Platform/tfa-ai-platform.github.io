Alternative Data

Importance of Alternative Data for Thin-file Users

Thin-file users are almost in the "blind spot of credit information" within CIC or JICC. By utilizing alternative data, it becomes possible to evaluate real-life payments and consumer behavior, enabling fairer credit scoring.

Utility Bill Payment History

Electricity, Water, Gas

Mobile Phone Data Plan Payment

Postpaid usage, ontime payments

Ewallet Transactions

PayPay, LinePay, Rakuten Pay, etc

Ecommerce Purchase History

Frequency, category mix, refund behavior

Rent Payments & Subscriptions

Netflix, gym memberships, etc
